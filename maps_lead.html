This story map was created in collaboration with Midwest Environmental Advocates and the Milwaukee-based Coalition on Lead Emergency (COLE) to document the socioeconomic causes and impacts of Milwaukee's lead crisis. It included interviews with Milwaukee community members directly impacted by lead contamination, such as landlords, public health professionals, and the parents of lead-poisoned children.
