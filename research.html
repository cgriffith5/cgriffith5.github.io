Current Research
My research interests include Landscape Studies, Public & Environmental Humanities, Legal Geography, and Cartography. My current research investigates legal geographies of property and inheritance in the rural Upper Midwest and Northern Great Plains. As a cartographer, I plan to produce a digital atlas that investigates the material and spatial qualities of the landscapes I conduct my dissertation fieldwork in.
